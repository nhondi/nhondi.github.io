Student, Game Developer, Web Designer


My name is Nathanael Hondi and I am currently a 18-year-old student situated in London at Queen Mary University studying Creative Computing. I am an aspiring games developer taking my passion for technology and using it to create a variety of games. "Currently, we are in a world where creativity and the desire to create is essential to bring something new to the world." Utilising Unity and C# I know how to program both 2D and 3D games and now seeks to learn a wide variety of programming languages to widen my skill set. I occasionally also does digital art, drawing different forms of fanart and portraits. These skills and interests are what set him on my path as a game developer.
